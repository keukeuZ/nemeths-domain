# Nemeths Domain - Captain System

## OVERVIEW

Every player controls a **Captain** - their avatar in the Domain. Captains are leaders who shape gameplay through class selection and one critical skill choice.

**Key Features:**
- 6 Classes with distinct roles
- 2 Skills per class (choose ONE)
- Skills are counters - your choice defines your strategy
- Locked for the entire generation
- New captain each generation (fresh start)

---

## CAPTAIN CREATION

### At Generation Start

1. **Choose Race** - Determines racial bonuses/penalties
2. **Choose Class** - Determines available skills
3. **Choose Skill** - A or B, locked for generation
4. **Name Captain** - Stored on-chain in Titan's Witness if you place

## CLASS 1: WARLORD

*Masters of warfare who lead from the front.*

### Base Abilities (All Warlords)
- **Commander's Presence**: Armies led by captain gain +5% ATK/DEF
- **Death Save Bonus**: +2 to captain death saves

### Skill A: Vanguard
*Offensive warfare - break their lines.*

**Effect:** First round of combat: +20% ATK. Siege weapons deal +25% damage.

**Counters:** Defensive turtles, wall-heavy players

**Countered by:** Oracle (sees attack coming), Fortress (negates first-round advantage)

### Skill B: Fortress
*Defensive mastery - hold the line.*

**Effect:** +25% DEF when defending owned territory. Buildings gain +20% HP.

**Counters:** Vanguard (negates first-round bonus), aggressive raiders

**Countered by:** Saboteur (destroys buildings), Destruction magic

---

## CLASS 2: ARCHMAGE

*Masters of the arcane who bend reality.*

### Base Abilities (All Archmages)
- **Mana Affinity**: +25% mana regeneration
- **Arcane Shield**: Once per day, negate one incoming spell

### Skill A: Destruction
*Offensive magic - burn them all.*

**Effect:** Damage spells deal +30% damage. Spell criticals cause fire DOT (50 damage/round, 3 rounds).

**Counters:** Massed armies, defensive positions, Fortress skill

**Countered by:** Protection skill, Oracle (predicts spell timing)

### Skill B: Protection
*Defensive magic - shields and wards.*

**Effect:** All friendly units gain +15% magic resistance. Can cast Ward on buildings (immune to next spell).

**Counters:** Destruction mages, spell-heavy strategies

**Countered by:** Assassin (physical damage ignores wards), Saboteur

---

## CLASS 3: HIGH PRIEST

*Servants of the Titan who channel divine power.*

### Base Abilities (All High Priests)
- **Divine Favor**: +10% to all D20 rolls
- **Blessing**: Once per day, remove one debuff from army

### Skill A: Crusader
*Holy warrior - smite the enemies.*

**Effect:** +20% ATK against Forsaken. Once per battle: one attack cannot miss. Unlocks Templar Guard unit.

**Counters:** Forsaken-heavy strategies, evasion builds

**Countered by:** Pack Alpha (swarm overwhelms), Saboteur (avoids direct combat)

### Skill B: Oracle
*Seer of the future - knowledge is power.*

**Effect:** See incoming attacks 2 hours earlier. Before battle: see probable outcome (win/lose/close). Once per day: know one player's next action.

**Counters:** Vanguard (no surprise attacks), Assassin (sees them coming)

**Countered by:** Saboteur (indirect damage), Destruction (can't dodge spells)

---

## CLASS 4: SHADOW MASTER

*Masters of subterfuge who strike from darkness.*

### Base Abilities (All Shadow Masters)
- **Stealth**: Armies can move undetected until within 3 tiles
- **Escape Artist**: +3 to captain death saves

### Skill A: Assassin
*Targeted elimination - remove key threats.*

**Effect:** 15% chance to force enemy captain death save on attack. Marked targets take +25% damage from all sources.

**Counters:** Captain-dependent strategies, Oracle (captain is vulnerable), Archmages (fragile)

**Countered by:** Fortress (protected captains), Warden (beasts protect captain)

### Skill B: Saboteur
*Destruction expert - break their things.*

**Effect:** +50% damage to buildings. Can instantly destroy bridges (50 mana, 24h cooldown). Traps deal 5% damage to invaders.

**Counters:** Fortress (destroys their buildings), economy-focused players

**Countered by:** Pack Alpha (doesn't rely on buildings), aggressive expansion

---

## CLASS 5: MERCHANT PRINCE

*Masters of commerce who buy victory.*

### Base Abilities (All Merchant Princes)
- **Trade Network**: +20% gold income
- **Logistics**: Army supply costs -10%

### Skill A: Profiteer
*War economy - profit from conflict.*

**Effect:** +30% loot from battles. +20% razing rewards. Earn gold equal to 5% of all damage dealt.

**Counters:** Expensive army builds, prolonged wars

**Countered by:** Aggressive early attacks (before economy builds), Saboteur

### Skill B: Artificer
*Technology and innovation - better tools.*

**Effect:** Building construction -25% time. Unit training -20% time. Siege weapons +20% damage.

**Counters:** Slow builders, defensive turtles (siege bonus)

**Countered by:** Rush strategies, Assassin (tech doesn't protect captain)

---

## CLASS 6: BEASTLORD

*Masters of beasts who command nature's fury.*

### Base Abilities (All Beastlords)
- **Animal Companion**: Personal beast grants +5% to one stat
- **Nature's Swiftness**: Army movement +10%

### Skill A: Pack Alpha
*Lead the hunt - overwhelm with numbers.*

**Effect:** Summon 20 wolf units daily (weak but free). +5% ATK per 100 units in army. Below 50% HP: army gains +25% ATK.

**Counters:** Small elite armies, quality-over-quantity builds

**Countered by:** Destruction (AOE kills swarms), Crusader (bonus vs beasts)

### Skill B: Warden
*Beast taming - command powerful creatures.*

**Effect:** Unlock War Beast unit (slow, tanky, hits hard). All beast units gain +15% stats. Beast units protect captain (redirects assassination attempts).

**Counters:** Assassin (beasts protect captain), glass cannon builds

**Countered by:** Destruction (magic kills beasts), Saboteur (beasts can't protect buildings)

## CAPTAIN DEATH

### When Can a Captain Die?

| Trigger | Condition |
|---------|-----------|
| **Army Destroyed** | Captain's army reaches 0 HP |
| **Critical Hit** | Natural 20 attack roll |
| **Assassination** | Assassin skill (15% chance per attack) |

### Death Save

When a death trigger occurs, the captain rolls D20:

| Roll | Result |
|------|--------|
| 1-9 | **Death** - Captain lost for this generation |
| 10-20 | **Survival** - Captain escapes, wounded |

### Death Save Modifiers

| Source | Modifier |
|--------|----------|
| Warlord class | +2 |
| Shadow Master class | +3 |
| Ashborn race | +2 |
| Defending in Fortress | +2 |
| Warden beasts | +2 |

**Maximum modifier:** +5 (cumulative)

### Consequences

**If captain survives:**
- Wounded for 24 hours
- Cannot lead armies during recovery
- Armies operate at -20% effectiveness

**If captain dies:**
- All captain bonuses lost permanently
- Armies function but with no captain bonuses
- Massive morale hit (-30%)
- Can still play, just severely weakened
